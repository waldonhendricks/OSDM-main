About Us

Our vision & mission statement

We are committed to teaching ministry skills that enable believers for relevant, meaningful and practical Christian Ministry.

Open Door School Of Ministry has had the privilege of training and equipping students from all types of local churches in South Africa.

Our students graduate from ODSOM as transformed individuals, by the Holy Spirit, maturing in the image of Christ and Manifesting the glory of God in their lives.
